Vision4robotics Group

Introduction

The vision4robotics group is a multidisciplinary research group at Tongji University. Our research interests include the following aspects that focus on intelligent vision and control technologies for robotics.

Interests

  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics
  • Machine Learning
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Intelligent Control

Five papers accepted by IROS 2020!

Our paper “DR^2Track: Towards Real-Time Visual Tracking for UAV via Distractor Repressed Dynamic Regression”, “Augmented Memory for Correlation Filters in Real-Time UAV Tracking”, “Automatic Failure Recovery and Re-Initialization for Online UAV Tracking with Joint Scale and Aspect Ratio Optimization”, “Towards Robust Visual Tracking for Unmanned Aerial Vehicle with Tri-Attentional Correlation Filters”, and “Learning Consistency Pursued Correlation Filters for Real-Time UAV Tracking” are accepted by IROS 2020 in Las Vegas, USA!
